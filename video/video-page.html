ASEAN Leaders to Discuss Key Regional Issues at the 42nd ASEAN Summit in Labuan Bajo


Jakarta, Indonesia - The 42nd ASEAN Summit is scheduled to commence soon. The series of events for the 42nd ASEAN Summit will be held on May 10-11, 2023, in Labuan Bajo, East Nusa Tenggara.

The meeting will be attended by Heads of State/Government of ASEAN member countries, including Timor-Leste as the 11th ASEAN member with observer status, in accordance with the results of the 40th and 41st ASEAN Summits in Cambodia.

The 42nd ASEAN Summit is the first of two ASEAN Summits under Indonesia's chairmanship of ASEAN in 2023. At the first Summit in May, the focus of discussion among the ASEAN Leaders will be on internal ASEAN issues and other important issues in and outside the region. While at the 43rd ASEAN Summit planned for September in Jakarta, the ASEAN Leaders and ASEAN's external partners will discuss the development and strengthening of ASEAN cooperation with its external partners.
